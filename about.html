About Us

The story of our company

Ecotrix was founded in 2010 with a mission to create a cleaner environment. At the initial launch, we began with small-scale residential recycling services, focusing on paper and plastics. Over time, we experienced significant growth and community recognition, earning trust for our reliable and eco-friendly solutions. To meet rising demands, we expanded into service diversification, offering commercial and industrial recycling, as well as e-waste and organic waste management. Through strategic partnerships with local businesses, municipalities, and environmental organizations, we strengthened our operations. Today, we stand as a leading recycling company dedicated to sustainability and innovation.
