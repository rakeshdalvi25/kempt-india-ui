About Us

Recycling services play an important role in keeping our environment clean and sustainable. These services help collect, sort, and process waste materials such as paper, plastic, glass, metals, and electronics so they can be reused instead of ending up in landfills. Residential recycling services provide bins and curbside collection for households, while commercial services support businesses by managing large volumes of packaging, paper, and food waste. Industrial recycling handles heavy materials like scrap metal, construction debris, and hazardous waste safely. By using recycling services, we save natural resources, reduce pollution, and create a greener future for the next generation. At Ecotrix, we are passionate about transforming spaces with high-quality recycling solutions. Recycling services come in various types depending on the material being recycled.
